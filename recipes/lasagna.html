Lasagna Recipe

Discription

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of alternating
layers of pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, cheese, and various
fillings, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat (typically beef or pork)
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Cheese (such as mozzarella, ricotta, or Parmesan)
  • Seasonings (such as garlic, oregano, and basil)
