Lasagna Recipe

Discription

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of alternating
layers of pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, cheese, and various
fillings, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat (typically beef or pork)
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Cheese (such as mozzarella, ricotta, or Parmesan)
  • Seasonings (such as garlic, oregano, and basil)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature (usually around 375°F or 190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground meat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce or marinara sauce to the cooked meat and stir well. Season with herbs and spices to taste.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the cheeses (mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan) to create the cheese filling.
  6. Grease a baking dish and begin layering the lasagna. Start with a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of the meat sauce, and then a layer of the cheese filling. Repeat the layers until you've used all the ingredients, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese on top is bubbly and golden.
  9. Once done, let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving to allow it to set.
  10. Slice and serve the delicious homemade lasagna!
